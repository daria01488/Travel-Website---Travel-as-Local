About Me

Hi! I’m Daria – a curious student, traveler, and local-life enthusiast. I believe the best way to explore a place is through the eyes of someone who lives there. That’s why I created Travel as Local – a space where I share real experiences, off-the-beaten-path spots, cozy food places, scenic walks, and everyday magic that tourists often miss.

Whether you’re heading to a buzzing capital or a quiet town, I want to help you feel connected — like you belong. I’m constantly exploring and collecting moments to help others discover more meaningful, down-to-earth ways to travel. Let’s turn your trip into something personal, soulful, and unforgettable.
