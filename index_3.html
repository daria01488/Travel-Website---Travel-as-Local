Cities

From cozy corners to vibrant neighborhoods, each city has its own rhythm and charm. Explore our handpicked highlights and local tips to experience the true heart of every place.

Berlin

Berlin is a haven for art lovers and night owls alike—its ever-changing street art, legendary techno clubs, and cozy coffee corners offer endless surprises around every corner.

Bologna

Bologna’s covered porticoes and medieval towers create an inviting atmosphere, while its world-famous pasta (especially tagliatelle al ragù) makes every meal a highlight.

Bratislava

Bratislava charms with its pastel-hued Old Town and hilltop castle views, and the family-run bakeries serve the loveliest plum strudels in Slovakia.

Budapest

Budapest shines with thermal baths set against grand architecture, plus atmospheric ruin bars in the Jewish Quarter where history meets hipster flair.

Florence

Florence unfolds like a Renaissance painting—its Duomo dome, Uffizi masterpieces, and sunset views over the Arno make it an art-lover’s dream.

Hamburg

Hamburg’s harborfront buzzes with maritime energy: fresh fish markets, the Elbphilharmonie’s wave-like design, and leafy Speicherstadt warehouses to explore.

Milan

Milan combines high fashion and hidden gems—expect world-class shopping on the Quadrilatero, plus quaint aperitivo bars tucked away in side streets.

Munich

Munich balances Bavarian tradition and modern life: sunny beer gardens, the annual Oktoberfest spirit, and green hills perfect for afternoon strolls.

Naples

Naples pulses with Mediterranean flavor—authentic Neapolitan pizza, lively street markets, and sweeping views of Vesuvius make it an unforgettable getaway.

Palermo

Palermo bursts with Sicilian warmth: vibrant street food markets, ornate Arab-Norman architecture, and seaside promenades lined with orange trees.

Prague

Prague enchants with its fairytale skyline of towers and bridges, Gothic alleys lit by lanterns, and hearty Czech pubs perfect for warming up on chilly evenings.

Rome

Rome’s layers of history come alive at every turn: ancient ruins such as the Colosseum, Baroque fountains like Trevi, and piazzas buzzing with café culture.

Venice

Venice floats on romance and intrigue—glide down misty canals by gondola, sample cicchetti in hidden bàcari, and admire St. Mark’s golden mosaics.

Vienna

Vienna dazzles with imperial elegance—grand palaces, candlelit coffeehouses serving Sacher torte, and a music-filled legacy from Mozart to Strauss.
