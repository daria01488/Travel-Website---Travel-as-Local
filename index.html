Travel with Heart

Welcome to Travel as Local – your guide to exploring the world not as a tourist, but as a curious friend. We believe that travel is more than just sights; it's the food shared with strangers, the alleyways full of stories, the quiet cafés where locals linger, and the thrill of spontaneity.

Whether you're wandering the hidden corners of Budapest or sipping wine in a quiet Vienna tavern, we offer tips, tales, and routes that connect you to the heart of each place. Every recommendation comes with a local touch – real, lived-in experiences that make you feel at home wherever you go.

What You’ll Find Here

Travel as Local is your guide to discovering Europe not through tourist brochures, but through real experiences, personal tips, and hidden treasures. Here's what you can explore:

Trips:

From Bucharest through Budapest to Vienna: Culture, Cafés & Cobblestones - Wander through Eastern Europe's charm — from vibrant city squares to soft pastries and quiet bookshops.
Hamburg, Berlin, Prague & Munich: Urban Charm with a Local Twist - Life explodes with energy here — riverside lounging, quirky neighborhoods, wild museums, and sunsets that stretch the days forever.
Italia from North to South: Milan to Palermo - Slow down and savor Italy — espresso in Milan, gondolas in Venice, art in Florence, and sunshine in Palermo.

Cities:

  • Berlin — Grit, graffiti, and history — a city that reinvents itself with every sunrise.
  • Bologna — Porticoes, pasta, and poetry in the heart of Italy’s culinary soul.
  • Bratislava — A small city with a big heart – walkable, whimsical, and full of charm.
  • Bucharest — A mix of bold history and soft green parks, with stories behind every turn.
  • Budapest — Grand architecture meets hidden ruin bars – a city to fall in love with, again and again.
  • Florence — Renaissance dreams under a golden Tuscan sky.
  • Hamburg — Canals, creativity, and the cool breeze of a harbor city with soul.
  • Milan — Where sleek fashion meets foggy mornings and lively piazzas.
  • Munich — Beer gardens, alpine air, and a perfect blend of tradition and tech.
  • Naples — Bold, flavorful, and bursting with soul – Italy’s fiery heart.
  • Palermo — Citrus air, mosaic streets, and the warm rhythm of the Mediterranean.
  • Prague — A fairy tale of spires and cobblestones wrapped in Bohemian mystery.
  • Rome — Ancient stones, fountains, and a glow that never fades.
  • Venice — Floating poetry — narrow canals, timeless beauty, and quiet wonder.
  • Vienna — Elegant and soulful, where time slows down with cake and coffee.
